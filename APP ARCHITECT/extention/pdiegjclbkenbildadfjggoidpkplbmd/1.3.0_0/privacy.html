Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

PromptCard helps users analyze images and generate structured prompts. This policy explains what data the extension handles when you use PromptCard.

What we collect

  • Account data you provide during sign-in, such as email address.
  • Image analysis data needed to generate prompt results.
  • Prompt history and usage counts when account or cloud features are enabled.
  • Basic product analytics such as installs, clicks, and analysis events.

How we use data

  • To generate prompt results for the image you choose.
  • To manage account sign-in, credit balance, and purchase records.
  • To provide support, billing, and product improvements.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not use your images or prompts for advertising targeting.

Third-party services

PromptCard may use model providers, analytics services, authentication providers, and payment processors to operate the extension.
